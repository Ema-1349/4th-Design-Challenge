Keeper of the Lost Cities

Shannon Messenger

Shannon Messenger is an American author and has her books published by Simon and Schuster She wrote the middle-grade fiction series Keeper of the Lost Cities, a New York Times bestseller. Keeper of the Lost Cities is an upper-middle-grade fantasy series that tells the story of Sophie Foster, a twelve-year-old high school senior who can read minds. She has always felt alone, until a mysterious boy named Fitz Vacker shows her that there is a place where she belongs, and staying in the human world will put her in terrible danger. She is forced to leave behind everything she has known and start a new life in the Lost Cities. Only then does she realize that she was given extraordinarily powerful abilities — and deadly secrets hidden in her subconscious — that some may kill for.

Sophie Foster

Regarded as the 'Mooklark', for project mooklark, Sophie Elizabeth Foster is a very special elf in the Elvin world. Born from humans in a mortal realm, she is 'kidnapped' to her new home-Havenfield and lives there as an elf with extrodinary powers that surpass anyone else. Forced to become accustomed to her surroudings, everything that she believed in is false, even her age. Her abundance of special abilities, along with her upbringing, makes her a remarkably talented elf, and targets her amongst rebelling groups. The Neverseen, rebel elves, are determinded to kidnap or kill her for their own uses, wrecking havoc in the Lost Cities. The Black Swan, however dangerous as ledgends say to be, will stop at nothing to protect Sophie and her group of friends from danger.

Black Swan

The Black Swan is a rebel group of elves and multiple different species united together to try and improve their world. They are the ones who 'created' Sophie and made Project Moonlark and are known to have a set of cipher runes to communicate secretly. The organization is led by a group of elves known as The Collective led by the mysterious man named Mr Forkle, who has multiple different skins and roles in the world.
